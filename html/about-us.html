Our Core Value
At The A List, we strive and thrive to offer “beyond the A” services
  • ACTIVE
    We oppose either sitting back or running behind the trend. Instead, we aim to make The A List name equal trend-setter and first runner in Vietnam’s influencer marketing industry. Hence, every employee at The A List is encouraged and empowered to raise their voices and take action immediately.
  • APPRECIATIVE
    The A List’s achievement never results from the effort of a single person. On the contrary, it requires valuable opportunities and collective efforts for us to touch amazing milestones. We contribute such efforts to our daring colleagues and respectful partners.
  • ACTION-ORIENTED
    We understand that in business, words matter. But as the old saying goes, “action speaks louder than words”. In order to break new grounds and conquer the next great milestones, we value timely action.
  • ATTITUDE-VALUED
    All The A List employees are encouraged to infuse every action with respect, progressiveness, mindful and nonjudgmental ears, as well as “strive for excellence” mindset. That is how we generate excitement, inspire collaboration and yield results.
  • ALIGNMENT
    We are a team with a target. With alignment and unity at the heart of our business, we synergize diverse expertises, knowledge, skills and talents to cultivate growth and drive fearlessly towards achievements.
